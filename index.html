About Us

At Godfery Okaye Teaching Hospital (GOUNITH), we are dedicated to providing exceptional healthcare services to our community. Our commitment to excellence and patient-centered care is at the core of everything we do.

  • Our Mission

    To deliver high-quality healthcare services that meet the needs of our diverse patient population. We strive to enhance the health and well-being of our community through compassionate care, advanced medical technology, and continuous improvement

  • Our Vision

    To be a leading healthcare provider known for excellence in clinical care, education, and research. We aim to create a healthier future for our community by fostering innovation and collaboration

Services

We offer comprehensive healthcare services tailored to meet the diverse needs of our patients with excellence and compassion.

Internal Medicine

We provide expert care for the prevention, diagnosis, and treatment of adult diseases, ensuring optimal health and well-being.

General Surgery

Our skilled surgeons perform a wide range of procedures with precision and care, from minor surgeries to complex operations.

Antenatal Care/Gynaecology

We offer comprehensive antenatal and gynecological services to support women's health at every stage of life.

Pediatrics

Our pediatric department provides specialized care for infants, children, and adolescents to ensure their healthy development.

Family Medicine

Our family medicine practitioners provide continuous and comprehensive care for individuals and families of all ages.

Cardiology

We offer advanced cardiac care services, including diagnostics, treatment, and rehabilitation for heart conditions.

Ophthalmology

Our ophthalmology department provides comprehensive eye care services, from routine exams to advanced surgical procedures.

Urology

Our urology specialists offer diagnosis and treatment for urinary tract and male reproductive system conditions.

Orthopedics

We provide comprehensive care for musculoskeletal conditions, including fractures, joint problems, and sports injuries.

Plastic Surgery

Our plastic surgery department offers reconstructive and cosmetic procedures to enhance appearance and function.

Psychiatry

We provide comprehensive mental health services, including diagnosis, treatment, and counseling for psychiatric conditions.

Nephrology

Our nephrology department offers advanced care for kidney conditions, including dialysis and transplant services.

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to some of the most common questions about our services and policies.

What services do you offer?

We provide a wide range of healthcare services, including general medicine, specialized treatments, and preventive care. Our goal is to meet all your healthcare needs with excellence and compassion.

Do you accept insurance?

Yes, we accept a variety of insurance plans. Please contact our billing department for more information on the specific insurance providers we work with and the coverage details.

What are your operating hours?

We are open Sunday through Saturday from 8:00 AM to 6:00 PM, and public holidays from 9:00 AM to 1:00 PM. For emergency services, we are available 24/7.

How can I contact you for more information?

You can reach us by phone, email, or through our website's contact form. Our contact details are available on the Contact Us page. We look forward to assisting you with any questions or concerns you may have.
